<h3>Kickstart your learning process of League Knowledge</h3>

<p>Champ 101 gives you a quick lookup for all the basic champion information you need if you<br>
<ul>
<li>are new to League or playing casually</li>
<li>are wondering what abilities of that rare picked champion actually do</li>
<li>are interested in your opponents cooldowns and ressource costs during your game</li>
</ul>
Fast, simple and relevant to your current game!</p>

<p>For a more detailed explanation of the app and its features visit <a href="http://champ101.krispin.it">the app's web-page</a></p>

<p>Feel free to give feedback here or <a href="mailto:champ101@krispin.it" target="_blank">send a message</a>
<br><br>
If you're using Github, consider opening an
<a href="https://github.com/Colorfulstan/overwolf-champ101/issues" target="_blank">issue</a>
for bugs and suggestions instead.</p>

<p><strong>Note:</strong> Despite our effort to work around cases of incosistency within the public datasets
used for the displayed information there might still be a few missing or wrong values.<br>
Values that we are unsure about are displayed with <span style="text-decoration: line-through">linethrough</span>.
They might still be correct, but don't bet on it. Where you see placeholders like {{ f3 }} no value
could be mapped at all. Please do not report those cases, since we collect them already.</p>
