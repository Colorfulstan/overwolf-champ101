Known Issues

  • No data will be available for games with spectators disabled or for bots

  • Despite our effort to work around cases of incosistency within the public datasets used for the displayed information there might still be a few missing or wrong values. Feel free to report those by email or open a github-issue including the champion and ability for reference.

  • Feel free to give feedback at the overwolf-store or send a message to champ101@krispin.it.

    If you're using Github, consider opening an issue for bugs and suggestions instead.
