Information

General Information

  • By Default the app will automatically start after the loading screen and close again when you leave the game

  • After you entered the game and the data is loaded, a small dock will show up on top of your screen. In the beginning, it has an attention pulse, so you don't miss it. Hover over it or use the ingame-hotkeys to make the pulse disappear

  • The match-window will automatically collapse if you click on the game. This behaviour is neccessary for Riot Games to allow the app

  • Closing a window will not end the app. If you closed the match window in game, you can bring it up again with the hotkey for minimizing/restoring

App Walkthrough

  • Set the summoner name within the settings    to a player who is in a game (or try three dashes)

  • Open the match window by clicking  

  • Click a portrait in the match window to open a champions panel. You can open up to 6 champion panels for quick access

  • Within the champion panels, click on an ability to play its video (if available)

Known Issues

  • No data will be available for games with spectators disabled or for bots

  • Hotkeys will not work outside of the game

  • Some videos are only available in unsupported format or the supported video might be out of date (for champion reworks)

  • Despite our effort to work around cases of incosistency within the public datasets used for the displayed information there might still be a few missing or wrong values. Feel free to report those by email or open a github-issue including the champion and ability for reference.

  • Feel free to give feedback at the overwolf-store or send a message to champ101@krispin.it.

    If you're using Github, consider opening an issue for bugs and suggestions instead.
